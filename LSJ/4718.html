Theoc.1.46; of ripe, fresh grapes, opp. ὄμφαξ on the one hand, and σταφίς on the other, AP5.303: collectively in sg., PPetr.3p.60 (iii B.C.), PCair.Zen.300.14 (iii B.C.), POxy.116.18 (ii A.D.), etc.
2. ς. ἀγρία,= “μήλωθρον” 1, Thphr.HP3.18.11, Plin.HN23.21.
