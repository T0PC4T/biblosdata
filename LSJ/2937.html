κτίσ-ις [ι^], εως, ἡ, （κτίζω)
A.founding, settling, Th.6.5; “ἀποικιῶν” Isoc. 12.190, cf. Plb.9.1.4 (pl.), etc.
2. loosely, = πρᾶξις, κούφα κ. an easy achievement, Pi.O.13.83.
3. creation, “κ. κόσμου” Ep.Rom. 1.20; “ἀπ᾽ ἀρχῆς κτίσεως” Ev.Marc.10.6, 13.19, etc.
II. created thing, creature, LXX Ju.9.12, Ev.Marc.16.15, Ep.Rom.8.19, etc.: in pl., LXX To.8.5.
III. authority created or ordained, 1 Ep.Pet.2.13.
