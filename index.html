Strong's Exhaustive Bible Concordance exegesis

The Strong's Exhaustive Concordance is the most complete, easy-to-use, and understandable concordance for studying the original languages of the Bible. The synopsis allows users to understand the particular derivation of the word.

Henry George Liddell & Robert Scott's Greek-English Lexicon (LSJ)

A Greek-English Lexicon (LSJ) is a standard lexicographical work of the Ancient Greek language originally edited by Henry George Liddell, Robert Scott, Henry Stuart Jones, and Roderick McKenzie and published in 1843 by the Oxford University Press.
